Where We Live - Android social app for a national bank

May 2017 - August 2017

For aspirational Millennials who want to make an impact on their communities and share their experiences, Where We Live is a mobile app that lets users identify issues and actions that will make a difference, chip in micro-donations, and earn cash by completing projects.

My Role

Android/Backend Developer:
In charge of working closely with UX designers and client to design the solutions, build the app's backend using Google cloud service, and integrate UI elements from sketches into the app.

My accomplishments

  • Created a fully functional prototype that has scalable back-end using Google cloud service (using non-relational databases and Node.js).
  • Improved app's performance by implementing lazy load, caches.
  • Helped the team work faster by sharing and teaching about Android development.

Challenges

  • The scope of the project was very large because our client is a national bank that has extremely large customer base and high expectation.
  • 4 out of 6 team members had no experience in Android development before.
  • Serverless API like Firebase Google cloud service can help develop the app faster but also has many limitations in managing server-side data.
