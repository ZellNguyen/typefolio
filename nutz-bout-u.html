Nutz 'Bout U - Mobile Game

Sep 2016 - Dec 2016

Nutz 'bout U is a 2D mobile game where the gameplay centers around your ability to climb up a structure, while avoiding objects thrown at you from various characters that live or work around the environment that become your enemies. You must find Sarah, who is usually located at the top most level of the structure.
