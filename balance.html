Balance - IOS Social Healthcare app

Sep 2016 - Jan 2017

Balance is a mobile application for anyone who is interested in a healthy lifestyle through balanced nutrition and physical activities. Unlike competitors, whose complicated tracking methods fail to give casual users practical recommendations and require them to form new habits, Balance involves users’ friends to help them with healthier choices and leverages on users’ daily activities, walking and photo taking.

My Role

IOS Developer:
In charge of working closely with UX designers and client to design the solutions, building the app's architecture, and developing it using Swift/XCode.

My accomplishments

  • From client's request, built the information architectures and class diagrams that demonstrated the user flow and data structure in the app.
  • Rapidly created and tested 3 prototypes in 4 weeks.
  • Handed the final deliverable for the client after only 10 weeks working.

Challenges

  • We had limited time (10 weeks), yet the every team member was very ambitious to build a fully functional app.
  • As the first term of the program, we were not familiar with the new working environment and culture.
  • MVC pattern was clumpsy, I tend toward using MVVM pattern after this project.
