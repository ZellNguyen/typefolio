Disruption - Interactive Narrative Game

Jan 2017 - Apr 2017

Disruption is a single player interactive narrative PC game that allows you to experience the psychological and real devastation caused by warfare. This surreal story offers a glimpse into the world of a soldier staving off post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), and considering a path of resistance.

My Role

Programmer, Technical artist:
In charge of programming the game and integrating the art assets made by the artists into Unity2D.
Video Editor:
Editing the cutscene videos for the game.

My accomplishments

  • Built a decision-based narrative framework that helped the team test, change and add new decision branch into the narrative easily.
  • Built a 2D object interaction framework that allowed artists to drag and drop the art assets into Unity2D without knowing coding.
  • Worked with the producer and artists to rapidly prototype and test the game in the short development cycles.
  • Created 5 cutscene videos for the game.
